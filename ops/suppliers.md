## Ordering Process

1. **Check Shopify inventory** — confirm restock is needed
2. **Email/portal order** — send SKU, quantity, delivery address
3. **Receive pro-forma invoice** — check items, quantities, price
4. **Pay deposit or full amount** as per agreed terms
5. **Confirm shipping method** — express or standard
6. **Track shipment** — note tracking in a spreadsheet or Shopify note
7. **Receive and inspect** — photograph items on arrival
8. **Update Shopify inventory**
9. **File invoice** (for VAT records — required by Belgian law)

---

## Quality Issues with Suppliers

If items arrive damaged or incorrect:

1. Photograph immediately (unopened if possible, then unpacked)
2. Email supplier within **48 hours** of receipt with photos
3. Negotiate: replacement, credit note, or partial refund
4. Keep records for potential chargeback if unresolved

---

## Supplier Evaluation Criteria

Rate suppliers annually on:

| Criterion | Weight |
|---|---|
| Product quality consistency | 30% |
| Lead time reliability | 25% |
| Communication responsiveness | 20% |
| Pricing competitiveness | 15% |
| Return/warranty policy | 10% |

---

## Dropshipping Notes

For products fulfilled directly by supplier (dropship model):

- Supplier ships directly to customer using **Kavoni Design** branding if possible
- Agree on **plain / white-label packaging** — no supplier branding
- Track all dropship orders separately
- Customer-facing lead time in product description must match supplier lead time
- Any supplier delay → **we communicate to customer** (not the supplier's job)
