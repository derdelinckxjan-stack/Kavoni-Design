## FAQ — Frequently Asked Questions

### Fitment & Compatibility

**Q: Will this fit my car?**
A: Every product listing specifies the exact chassis codes and year ranges it fits. Double-check the chassis code of your car — you can find it on the vehicle registration document or in the door jamb sticker. If unsure, email us with your chassis code and we'll confirm.

**Q: My car has an LCI facelift — does it still fit?**
A: We note LCI compatibility in each product listing. If the listing says "LCI compatible" or covers the facelift year range, you're covered. If not listed, contact us before ordering.

**Q: I have an M Performance or Competition variant — does fitment change?**
A: Sometimes. M Performance bumpers and Competition body kits can have different splitter/diffuser mounting points. We note M Performance and Competition fitment where relevant. Contact us if you're unsure.

---

### Orders & Delivery

**Q: How long will my order take?**
A: In-stock items ship within 1–2 business days. Delivery times depend on your zone — see `store/shipping.md`. You'll receive a tracking email once dispatched.

**Q: Can I change or cancel my order?**
A: If your order hasn't been dispatched yet, yes. Email us immediately at kavonidesign@gmail.com with your order number. Once dispatched, we cannot cancel — you'd need to return the item.

**Q: My tracking shows no movement — what do I do?**
A: Tracking can take 24–48 hours to update after dispatch. If there's no movement after 3 business days, contact us and we'll investigate with the carrier.

**Q: I received the wrong item.**
A: We're sorry. Email us at kavonidesign@gmail.com with your order number and a photo of what you received. We'll arrange a free return and send the correct item immediately.

---

### Returns & Refunds

**Q: Can I return an item I've already fitted?**
A: No. Once an item has been installed, we can no longer accept it as a return unless it is defective. Please test fitment before installation.

**Q: How do I start a return?**
A: Email kavonidesign@gmail.com with your order number and reason for return. We'll send instructions. Do not ship anything back without confirmation from us first.

**Q: When will I receive my refund?**
A: Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we process the refund within 5–7 business days. It will appear on your original payment method.

---

### Products & Technical

**Q: Is the product carbon fibre or carbon look?**
A: We clearly state the material in each listing. "Carbon fibre" = genuine woven carbon. "Carbon look" or "ABS carbon" = plastic with carbon-pattern finish. If unclear, ask us before ordering.

**Q: Does the exhaust come with a fitting kit / gaskets?**
A: Most systems include hardware. Check the product description's "In the box" section. If not listed, email us.

**Q: Will fitting headlights or taillights trigger a warning light?**
A: LED lighting sometimes requires a CANBUS resistor or a coding change via ISTA/ESYS to suppress error messages. We note this in product listings where applicable. For peace of mind, have a BMW specialist code the car after fitting.

**Q: Do you offer installation?**
A: We are an online retailer — we don't offer installation services. We recommend using a specialist BMW workshop or bodyshop for fitting.
