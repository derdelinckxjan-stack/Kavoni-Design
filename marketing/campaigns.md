## Active Campaigns

### Welcome Flow (Klaviyo — Evergreen)
- **Type:** Automated email flow
- **Trigger:** Newsletter sign-up via website form
- **Incentive:** Mystery gift — customer chooses between **10% off** (`WELCOME10`) or **free shipping** (`FREESHIP1`)
- **Flow structure:**
  1. Email 1 (immediate): Welcome + gift reveal + discount code delivery (conditional split based on `gift_choice` property)
  2. Email 2 (Day 3): Brand story / bestsellers
  3. Email 3 (Day 7): Social proof / model-specific recommendations *(to be built)*
- **Status:** Flow 1 live. Emails 2–3 pending.
- **Results:** *(update with open rate, CTR, conversion once data accumulates)*

## Campaign Ideas Pipeline

### Idea 1 — Model-Specific Email Sequences
- **Concept:** Segment subscribers by their BMW model (captured at signup or via post-purchase tag) and send tailored product recommendations
- **Segments needed:** M3 owners, M4 owners, 3 Series owners, etc.
- **Platform:** Klaviyo segments + flow branches
- **Priority:** High — high relevance = high conversion
- **Status:** Idea stage

---

### Idea 2 — Seasonal Campaign: Summer Build Season
- **Concept:** "Build season" push in April–June when people work on their cars
- **Offer:** Aero bundle discount or free shipping on orders over €X
- **Content:** Before/after car builds, model-specific lookbooks
- **Channels:** Email (Klaviyo) + social (Instagram/Facebook)
- **Status:** Idea stage

---

### Idea 3 — Abandoned Checkout Recovery
- **Concept:** Automated email series for customers who add to cart but don't complete purchase
- **Platform:** Klaviyo Abandoned Cart flow (or Shopify native)
- **Email 1 (1 hour):** Reminder — "You left something behind"
- **Email 2 (24 hours):** Social proof / FAQ reassurance
- **Email 3 (72 hours):** Optional small incentive (free shipping?)
- **Status:** Not yet built — high priority

---

### Idea 4 — Post-Purchase Upsell Sequence
- **Concept:** After a customer buys exhaust tips, email them matching rear diffuser or grille
- **Logic:** Tag order with product category → trigger cross-sell flow
- **Platform:** Klaviyo post-purchase flow
- **Status:** Idea stage

---

### Idea 5 — BMW Event / Track Day Sponsorship
- **Concept:** Sponsor a local BMW club trackday or car meet with branded goods / discount codes
- **Goal:** Brand awareness + email list growth
- **Target events:** Belgian BMW clubs, Track days at Zolder / Spa
- **Status:** Research stage

---

### Idea 6 — User-Generated Content (UGC) Campaign
- **Concept:** Ask customers to share photos of their cars with Kavoni parts fitted
- **Incentive:** Feature on Instagram + entry into monthly prize draw
- **Hashtag:** #KavoniDesign or #KavonieOpDeWeg *(TBD)*
- **Status:** Idea stage
