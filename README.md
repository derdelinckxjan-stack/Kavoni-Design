## What We Sell

Kavoni Design supplies **BMW performance parts and accessories** across five product pillars:

- **Aerodynamics** — front splitters, rear diffusers, side skirts, spoilers (carbon fibre & composite)
- **Exhaust systems** — cat-back, axle-back, valved systems for turbo & NA engines
- **Lighting** — headlights, taillights, DRL upgrades
- **Interior & controls** — steering wheels, trim pieces, paddle shifters
- **Engine bay** — dress-up kits, protection covers

**161 products** across **21 BMW models** spanning E, F, and G Series. See [`models/`](./models/) for full coverage.
