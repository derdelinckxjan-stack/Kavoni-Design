## Shipping Policies

### Processing Time
- **In stock:** 1–2 business days before dispatch
- **On order / drop-ship:** 5–14 business days (stated in product description)
- Orders placed before **13:00 CET** on a weekday are processed same day

### Delays
- During sales/promotions, processing may extend by 1–3 days
- Carrier delays outside our control — we communicate proactively via email

### Lost or Damaged Parcels
1. Customer reports within **7 days** of expected delivery
2. We open a carrier claim
3. Replacement dispatched or refund issued within **10 business days** of claim resolution

### Free Shipping Threshold
- Applied at cart/checkout automatically by Shopify
- Threshold is **per order**, not per item
- Discount codes (e.g. `FREESHIP1`) override the threshold and grant free shipping on any order value
