## Aerodynamics

### Front Splitters & Lips
- Fitment-specific, model-year verified
- Materials: carbon fibre, ABS, polyurethane (PU)
- Finish: gloss carbon, matte carbon, gloss black, unpainted
- Mounting: OEM-style clips / adhesive / bolt-on depending on model

### Rear Diffusers
- Diffuser extensions and full replacements
- Carbon fibre and PP options
- Sport exhaust cut-outs available for dual/quad tip fitments

### Side Skirts
- Pair (LH + RH) always sold together
- OEM-replica and aggressive street styles
- Colour-coded painting service: customer provides paint code *(note in order)*

### Rear Spoilers
- Trunk lip spoilers and roof spoilers
- Carbon fibre and ABS options
- Self-adhesive or drill-fit mounting

### Grilles
- Front kidney grilles — gloss black, carbon look, chrome delete
- Clip-in and bolt-in variants per model
- M-style double-bar and mesh designs

## Exhaust Systems

### Cat-Back Systems
- Full system from cat to tips
- Sound levels: street (moderate), sport (louder), race (track use)
- Materials: stainless steel 304 / 316, titanium on select models

### Valved Exhaust Systems
- Electronically or vacuum-operated valve
- Two modes: quiet (daily) / open (sport)
- Compatible with OEM valve controller on select BMW models

### Exhaust Tips
- Bolt-on / weld-on fitments
- Finishes: polished, brushed, carbon fibre surround
- Sizes: 90mm, 100mm, 120mm round; 135mm oval
